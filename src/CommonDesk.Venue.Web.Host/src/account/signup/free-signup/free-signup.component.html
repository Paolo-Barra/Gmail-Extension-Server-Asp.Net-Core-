The process of creating your account requires ReFind to access your email account using OAuth credentials.

  1. We will your Gmail or Microsoft Auth Login as the primary login for the ReFind Website
  2. Using your credentials we will monitor all the messages you recieve
  3. We will scan your email history to determine the best ways for you to manage your email.
