reFind for Email is the solution

reFind is a SaaS application for SMB and Enterprise customers to automate and prioritize corporate email.  Within minutes, reFind will fully re-organize your Inbox and eliminate the need for routine daily filing of messages. Important messages are made visible, and less important messages are filed for later review.  reFind enhances your favorite Desktop email applications (Gmail, Outlook, and Office365) and runs on your mobile devices.
